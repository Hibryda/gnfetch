Bundled background images
=========================

All images in this directory are NASA / public-domain imagery, downscaled to
<=1600px and re-compressed (JPEG q82) for bundling. NASA media is generally not
copyrighted and may be used freely; see NASA's media usage guidelines:
https://www.nasa.gov/nasa-brand-center/images-and-media/
